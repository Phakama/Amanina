About Amanina Stationery

The company was established in 2014. With its head office based in Cape Town, the company has sufficient capacity to cater for the greater Cape Town region. Amanina Traders Co-op was founded by Nomfundo Macuphe and five other women bringing a more cost-effective and convenient way for parents to purchase stationery for their children without having to shop for each item individually or purchase the wrong item in error.

Our hope and the ultimate goal are to create a platform where parents can spend more quality family time instead of shopping around for stationery and where teachers spend less time trying to ensure that the children have all the correct stationery. We, therefore, have a dedicated team who are ready to assist all our customers in fulfilling every order.

Quality & Value

We have one of the most comprehensive ranges of stationery supplies and stationery from leading brands as well as vast range of quality products from independent suppliers and we really do have everything you need for your school including, back to school kits, envelopes, pens, paints as well as an impressive range of items the school may need to use on a daily basis.


We have hundreds of quality products and you'll find jut what you're looking for and our excellent sales team are always available to offer to advise and support whenever you need it.
